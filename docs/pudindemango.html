Mango pudding

Ingredients
  • Gelatine
  • 1 mango
  • Mil or cream
  • Sugar and salt
  • small bowls
steps
  1. Dissolve a packet of gelatin in boiled hot water.
  2. Stir in some sugar and a pinch of salt.
  3. Add in the milk or cream.
  4. Mix in the mango puree.
  5. Pour the mixture into 4 ramekins or small bowls.
  6. Add the corn, cilantro, green onions, and the guascas. Simmer, covered, for 20 minutes, or until potatoes are tender, but not overcooked.
  7. Remove the cilantro and green onions and return the chicken to the pot. Simmer a few minutes more until the chicken is warmed through.
  8. Cover with saran wrap and chill in the fridge for 2 hours or until set.
